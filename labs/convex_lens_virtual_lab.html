Key Concepts & Formulas

Ray Diagram Rules

  • Ray parallel to axis passes through focus.
  • Ray through optical center passes undeviated.
  • Ray through focus becomes parallel to axis.
  • Dashed lines represent virtual extensions.

Essential Formulas
