Key Concepts & Formulas

Key Concepts

  • Ohm's Law: Current (I) is directly proportional to voltage (V).
  • Current is inversely proportional to resistance (R).
  • Ohmic conductors show a linear V-I relationship (straight line).
  • Resistance depends on the material, length, and temperature.

Essential Formulas

V = IR P = VI P = I²R E = Pt

Variable Meanings

  • V = Voltage (Volts)   I = Current (Amperes)
  • R = Resistance (Ohms)   P = Power (Watts)
